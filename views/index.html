HOW IT WORKS

Real-Time Data Collection: Our sensors diligently track your plant's environment, noting the temperature, humidity, and moisture levels. This data is sent to our system using the reliable MQTT protocol.
Data Processing and Storage: Upon receiving the data, we store it securely in our database, ensuring every reading is logged for detailed tracking of your plant's health.
AI-Powered Insights: Here's where the magic happens! Our AI, powered by OpenAI, analyzes each reading and generates a witty, insightful comment about your plant's current condition. Think of it as having a plant-savvy friend who never misses a beat.
User-Friendly Dashboard: All this information is presented on a sleek, intuitive dashboard. You can view the latest readings and AI comments, offering both fun and practical insights into your plant's wellbeing.

Whether you're a green thumb or a plant newbie, our system makes plant care engaging, informative, and a tiny bit whimsical!
